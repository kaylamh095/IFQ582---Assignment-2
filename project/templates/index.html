Cultural Advisory Notice

We acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the Country throughout Australia and honour their Elders past and present.

This collection contains Indigenous Knowledge and History. Items are reviewed in collaboration with Indigenous community elders before they are made publicly available.

Users should be aware that collection items may contain images, voices or names of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people who have passed away.

About the Collection

The Library of Indigenous Knowledge and History provides access to items documenting Indigenous stories, histories and creative expressions.

The collection features items that have been reviewed by a Community Elder before being made publicly available. This process supports respectful access to culturally significant material and helps to ensure sensitive items are handled with proper care.
